1. Stern (2021)
Title: Analysis of Music Genre Clustering Algorithms

Context: This is a Master's Thesis from the University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee that explicitly compares unsupervised K-Means and Hierarchical clustering against supervised baselines on the FMA dataset.

Link: Access Thesis via UWM Digital Commons
https://www.google.com/search?q=https://minds.wisconsin.edu/bitstream/handle/1793/92824/Stern_uwm_0263M_13066.pdf 

2. Joffe (2023)
Title: Does Genre Mean Anything: Classifying Music with Artificial Intelligence

https://www.wbu.edu/academics/schools/school-of-math-and-science/undergraduate-research/documents/Joffe%202023.pdf 

Context: A research project (Wayland Baptist University) investigating K-Means and Agglomerative clustering on the GTZAN dataset. It highlights the "low purity" issue where unsupervised clusters often fail to map 1:1 with human-defined genres.

Link: Access PDF (WBU)

3. Patra & Das (Reference Correction)
Title: Mood Based Music Categorization System for Bollywood Music

Context: While often cited in 2023/2024 literature reviews (e.g., in surveys of Indian music classification), the foundational paper for this specific "Bollywood Mood Clustering" method was published earlier. This remains the primary reference for unsupervised mood clustering in Hindi music.

Link: Access via ACCENTS Journals
https://accentsjournals.org/PaperDirectory/Journal/IJACR/2014/3/29.pdf
